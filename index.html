Our Approach

Architecture
as a force
for good.

We are Elegant Designs — a Dar es Salaam practice that refuses to treat sustainability as a constraint. For us, it is the creative brief. Every building we produce demonstrates that ecological architecture can be more ambitious, more beautiful, and more enduring than anything that came before it.

Our 24-person team of architects, engineers, and ecologists takes on 6 to 8 projects per year. We work across East and West Africa because this is where the world's most consequential design decisions are being made right now.

Learn more about us
01

Climate-First Design

Before a line is drawn, we study the land. Solar path, wind, rainfall, soil — the site is our first design partner. Every building responds to its ecosystem rather than imposing upon it.

02

Buildings That Live

Rooftop gardens, living walls, biodiversity corridors — our structures are ecosystems. An Elegant Designs building actively increases habitat and sequesters carbon over its lifetime.

03

Honest Materials

Rammed earth. Reclaimed steel. Cross-laminated timber. We specify what we mean. Honest materials carry lower embodied carbon — and far more inherent beauty.

How We Work

Five stages.
Zero shortcuts.

Every project follows the same rigorous sequence — from the first site walk to the final planting. We stay on every project, every step. The ecological intent of the design survives contact with reality because we make sure it does.

01

Site & Climate Analysis

We read the land before drawing anything. Solar path, wind, rainfall, soil composition — the site tells us what's possible and responsible.

02

Concept & Carbon Modelling

Every sketch is tested against a carbon budget from day one. Net zero is the constraint, not an afterthought at planning sign-off.

03

Material Specification

Local, reclaimed, honest. We refuse any material whose extraction harms more than its use saves. Supply chains transparent by design.

04

Construction

We stay on site. Our architects build alongside teams, ensuring the ecological intent of the design survives contact with the real world.

05

Post-Occupancy Care

Reviews at 6 months and 2 years. Energy monitoring, performance tracking, and an ongoing relationship with every building we make.
